About Us

Our organization was established in 2007 by Mrs. Elie Simpson with the co-operation of Mr. Andi Shubert and Mrs. Clara Shubert in hopes of providing a better foundation for your child’s education while ensuring that they have fun. We are a board-certified child friendly service certified by the Ministry of Child care and Safety and also the Ministry of Health, that we provide you with the best service while caring for your child’s safety and health. We have chosen an optimum environment which is child-friendly and provides peace and calm for your child. With a library and play area, we provide all the fun and education that your child needs, all under one roof. Our experienced staff of care-takers will look after your child with the utmost care making sure that every child receives the proper amount of attention needed.

Open Hours

We are open on all weekdays, Monday through Friday, from 6:00am to 6:00pm, except for all mercantile holidays.
